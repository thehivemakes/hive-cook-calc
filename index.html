About CookCalc

CookCalc helps home cooks and professional chefs with kitchen math. Scale recipes up or down, convert between grams and cups, swap baking pan sizes, adjust servings, and calculate the cost per plate.

All tools run in your browser. Share a scaled recipe via URL — text it to your sous chef or bookmark it for next time.
