Converting Between Baking Pan Sizes

When you swap pans, you need to adjust the recipe quantity to maintain the same batter depth. The multiplier is the ratio of the new pan area to the original pan area.

A 9-inch round pan has about 64 square inches of area. An 8-inch square pan has 64 square inches too — so they are interchangeable without adjusting the recipe!
