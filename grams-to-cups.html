Converting Grams to Cups (and Back)

Cups and grams are not directly interchangeable because different ingredients have different densities. One cup of flour weighs about 125 grams, while one cup of sugar weighs about 200 grams.

For precise baking, weigh ingredients in grams with a kitchen scale. This converter helps when a recipe uses one system and you need the other.
