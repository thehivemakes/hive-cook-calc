How to Calculate Cost Per Serving

Knowing your cost per serving helps with meal planning and budgeting. Add up the cost of all ingredients used, then divide by the number of servings.

To find the cost of a partial ingredient (like half a bag of flour), divide the package price by the total amount, then multiply by the amount used.
