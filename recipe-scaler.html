How to Scale a Recipe

To scale a recipe, divide the desired number of servings by the original number. This gives you the scale factor. Multiply every ingredient by that factor.

Cooking recipes scale well at any ratio. Baking recipes are more sensitive — especially leavening agents (baking powder, yeast) and eggs. For large batch baking, test the scaled recipe once before committing.
